Cocktail

A cocktail is a mixed drink typically made with a distilled spirit and other ingredients like fruit juices, flavored syrups, or bitters. They are often alcoholic, but mocktails are non-alcoholic versions. Classic cocktails include the Margarita, Mojito, and Old Fashioned.

Evan Williams Bourbon Whiskey

Evan Williams Bourbon is a popular American whiskey produced by Heaven Hill Distillery in Bardstown, Kentucky. It's named after Evan Williams, who is credited with opening Kentucky's first commercial distillery in 1783.
